If there's one thing Amazon excels at, it would be having terrible, unorganized management. My training for using a heavy piece of machinery that is very capable of causing lots of damage to myself and others was one shift long, and about maybe an hour or two actually driving. I got pretty good at it, to where having one month experience put me above most other drivers.
